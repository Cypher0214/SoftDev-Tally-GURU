About Us

Softdev Tally Guru is a leading computer institute dedicated to providing high-quality education and training in the field of computer science and technology. With a team of experienced and dedicated faculty members, we offer a wide range of courses and programs to help students develop the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in the rapidly evolving field of technology.

At Softdev Tally Guru, we are committed to providing a supportive and inclusive learning environment where students can thrive and reach their full potential. Our state-of-the-art facilities and cutting-edge technology ensure that students have access to the latest tools and resources to enhance their learning experience.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a nation of highly skilled individuals, fostering innovation, economic growth, and social progress. By providing accessible and quality skill training.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower and nurture the careers of over 50,000 individuals through the cultivation of in-demand skills by the year 2024. We are dedicated to assisting them in realizing their aspirations and reaching their desired career destinations.
